Powernet is an open-source and open architecture platform for scalable and secure coordination of consumer flexible load and DERs.

VADER

Visualization and Analytics of Distributed Energy Resources (VADER) is a unified data analytics platform that enables the integration of massive and heterogenous data streams for granular real-time monitoring, visualization and control of Distributed Energy Resources (DER) in distribution networks.

VISDOM (Visualization and Insight for Demand Operations and Management) is a web-based platform and architecture for load analysis and management.
